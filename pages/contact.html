FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a room?

Visit the Room Booking page, choose your room type, and fill in the application form. We'll confirm within 24 hours.

What documents are required?

You'll need your student ID, admission letter, a passport photo, and a government-issued ID (Aadhaar/Passport).

Is food included in the rent?

Mess charges are separate. A monthly mess plan costs ₹2,500 and covers breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

What is the refund policy?

Security deposits are refunded within 30 days of check-out, subject to room inspection. Monthly rent is non-refundable.

World-Class Amenities

Designed to support your academic journey and personal well-being.

High-Speed Wi-Fi

1 Gbps fiber internet across all floors and common areas. Dedicated bandwidth per student ensures lag-free streaming and video calls.

  • 1 Gbps fiber connection
  • Available 24/7
  • Secure WPA3 encryption
  • IT support on call

Multi-layered security with CCTV, biometric access, and trained guards ensure your safety around the clock.

Quiet, air-conditioned study rooms with whiteboards, power outlets, and high-speed internet. Open 24/7.

  • Open 24/7

On-call nurse available daily. First-aid kits on every floor and tie-ups with nearby hospitals for emergencies.
